About Me

I am a researcher based in Munich, Germany, focusing on AI Safety, Adversarial Machine Learning, and Neuromorphic Vision. My work bridges intelligent perception, robust learning, and security for trustworthy AI systems.

My research explores adversarial trigger generation, reward model security, and real-time intrusion detection systems for automotive applications. I’m passionate about making AI systems not just powerful, but also secure, reliable, and trustworthy.

I completed my Master of Science in Cyber Security at Hochschule der Bayerischen Wirtschaft (HDBW), where I gained expertise in cloud security, network security, and advanced machine learning.
