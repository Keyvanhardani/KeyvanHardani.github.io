Biography

I am an AI researcher and engineer specializing in Computer Vision, AI Safety, and Document Intelligence. My research focuses on bridging the gap between theoretical AI safety concepts (Adversarial ML) and their robust application in high-compliance environments.

Currently, I operate as an independent Lead AI Engineer, designing specialized OCR and vision architectures for complex industrial domains (Engineering, Legal, Finance). Previously, I served as a Project Engineer for System & Integration Testing at MAN Truck & Bus SE, where I led the HiL Security team and focused on automotive cybersecurity. I hold an M.Sc. in Cyber Security from HDBW Munich.
